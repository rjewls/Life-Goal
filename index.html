🇩🇿 Algeria Papers & Work Abroad

Master plan: documents, military service options, CV templates, and routes to Gulf / Germany / Canada.

Master Plan: Get Your Papers & Work Abroad

Profile: 25 y/o male • Baccalaureate • 1 year college • 2 years Noest Express (Dispatch Agent / Station Supervisor)

⚠️ 3 Critical Blockers — Fix These First
  1. Recensement / Military service status — visit your commune or military recruitment office and get a written attestation. If "insoumis", ask for the official regularization procedure. MDN — Recensement ↗
  2. Passport (biometric) — ensure at least 12–24 months remaining. Book appointment online. Portail Passeport Biométrique ↗
  3. Work proof / CNAS — request an attestation de travail (signed & stamped) from Noest Express. Get your CNAS relevé de carrière. ELHANAA (CNAS portal) ↗

First 14 Days — Action Checklist

  • Make 3 copies of ID, acte de naissance (12S), CNAS card, passport page (scan PDFs)
  • Send HR attestation request to your manager today — ask for stamp & signature
  • Book appointments: passport office (if renewing), recruitment office (service national), CNAS local office
  • Create a folder on your phone & cloud named Papiers — Export with scanned copies

Military Service Options

🚨 The "Carte Jaune" is your #1 blocker. Border police will not let you exit without a card or a "Sursis" (deferment).
  • Option A (Fastest): Medical Exemption — get a specialist report for any chronic condition and submit to military tribunal.
  • Option B (Student Loophole): Enroll in CFPA or INSFP vocational school (Feb or Sept session) → get "Certificat de Scolarité" → new Sursis valid for travel. Choose "Transport & Logistics" or "English Language" to boost your CV.

Script — Approaching the Recruitment Office

Bonjour, je m'appelle [Ton Nom], né le [JJ/MM/AAAA]. Je voudrais régulariser mon recensement / connaître mon statut au service national et obtenir l'attestation de recensement nécessaire pour voyager. Quels documents dois-je fournir exactement et quelles sont les étapes à suivre ? Merci.

Attestation de Travail — Template to Give HR

[Entête de l'entreprise] Attestation de travail Je soussigné(e), [Nom du signataire], en qualité de [Fonction], certifie que M. [TON NOM] né le [JJ/MM/AAAA] a travaillé pour la société Noest Express du [Date de début] au [Date de fin / présent] en qualité de : - Agent d'expédition (Dispatch Agent) — traitement des colis à l'aide de lecteur manuel (hand scanner). - Responsable de station / Station Supervisor — gestion de la caisse, remises d'espèces aux clients, coordination des expéditions et gestion d'équipe. Cette attestation est délivrée à la demande de l'intéressé pour servir et valoir ce que de droit. Fait à [Ville], le [Date]. Signature & tampon

CV Bullets (English) — Copy-Paste Ready

Station Supervisor — Noest Express (MM/YYYY — Present) • Managed station cash flow up to [amount] DZD daily; reconciled receipts and performed refunds. • Processed 200–400 packages daily using handheld barcode scanners; coordinated dispatch and prioritized urgent shipments. • Supervised and trained a team of [X] couriers; handled client escalations and customer service. • Kept inventory logs, prepared daily reports, and liaised with regional operations. • Languages: French (native), Arabic (native), English (intermediate)

Destination Routes

🌍 Gulf (UAE / Qatar) — Fastest Route
Logistics/warehouse roles hire quickly; English + experience often beats a degree.
  • Apply remotely: Aramex Careers ↗, DHL Careers ↗, GulfTalent ↗, Bayt.com ↗, Indeed UAE ↗
  • Negotiate visa sponsorship after job offer — avoid agencies charging large upfront fees
  • UAE Embassy visas: mofa.gov.ae ↗
  • Germany — Ausbildung / Chancenkarte: Learn German (B1/B2), apply for "Fachkraft für Lagerlogistik" apprenticeship. Permanent residency pathway.
  • Canada — PNP: Saskatchewan / Alberta provincial nominees. Target logistics roles with LMIA employer support.

👋 Welcome to Life Advisor

Here's a quick tour of what this app can do for you.

1

Set Your Monthly Income

Go to Savings & Costs and enter your net monthly income. This enables automatic surplus calculation.

2

Add Your Recurring Costs

Add all monthly expenses (rent, food, transport, subscriptions) as cost items with "Recurring Monthly" checked.

3

Track Your Savings

Add a recurring monthly savings transfer as a saving item. One-off saving deposits also count toward your all-time savings total.

4

Read the Life Guides

Browse the consolidated guides — passport, military service, e-commerce, AI tools, and coding roadmap — with copy-paste templates.

5

Backup Your Data

Use "Save Snapshot" to create a timestamped backup in the backups/ folder. Export JSON to share or transfer data.
